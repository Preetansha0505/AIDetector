# 🧠 Step 0: Define the System Properly (Most people mess this up)

## 🎯 Goal

> Given a piece of text, output a **probability that it is AI-generated or AI-assisted**, along with explanations.

## ⚠️ Constraints

* Must work on **unknown models** (not just GPT)
* Must handle **paraphrased AI text**
* Must minimize **false accusations (false positives)**

# 🧠 Step 3: Feature Extraction (CORE of your system)

You are building **multiple detectors**, not one.

---

## 🔹 3.1 Perplexity Module

**Goal:** Measure predictability

## How:

* Use GPT-2

## Output:

```python
{
  "avg_perplexity": float,
  "perplexity_variance": float
}
```

---

## 🔹 3.2 Stylometry Module

**Goal:** Capture writing fingerprint

## Features:

* avg sentence length
* variance in sentence length
* type-token ratio
* stopword ratio
* POS tag distribution

---

## 🔹 3.3 Burstiness Module

**Goal:** Detect uniform vs irregular writing

## Features:

* std deviation of sentence length
* punctuation frequency variance

---

## 🔹 3.4 Repetition & Redundancy

## How:

* Use embeddings from
  sentence-transformers

## Compute:

* cosine similarity between sentences
* repetition score

---

## 🔹 3.5 Semantic Diversity

**Goal:** Measure idea richness

## Features:

* embedding dispersion
* topic spread

---
